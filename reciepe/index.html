Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, beat the egg and then add the milk, melted butter (or oil), and vanilla extract if using. Mix well.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.
  4. Heat a pan and pour batter to form pancakes
  5. Cook until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Serve warm with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.
